Privacy Policy for Sheet Viewer Editor

Last updated: 26/11/2024

1. Introduction

Sheet Viewer Editor ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our Google Workspace application.

2. Information We Collect

When you use Sheet Viewer Editor, we access:

  • Your Google Sheets data (only the sheets you explicitly share with our application)
  • Basic Google account information for authentication

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information solely to:

  • Display and edit your Google Sheets data as requested
  • Save your preferences (such as last used sheet ID)
  • Enable the functionality of the application

4. Data Storage

  • We do not store any of your Google Sheets data on our servers
  • All data processing happens in real-time through Google's APIs
  • Your sheet preferences are stored locally in your browser

5. Data Sharing

We do not:

  • Share your data with third parties
  • Store or process your data outside of Google's infrastructure
  • Use your data for any purpose other than providing the service

6. Security

We protect your data by:

  • Using Google's secure OAuth 2.0 protocol for authentication
  • Processing data only within Google's secure infrastructure
  • Never storing sensitive information

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Revoke access to your Google Sheets at any time
  • Request information about how your data is used
  • Remove all locally stored preferences

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.
